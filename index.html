About

Dr. Sanjim completed her MBBS (2006) and MD Medical Microbiology (2010) from Maulana Azad Medical College, New Delhi 2010. She has over 9 years of experience in various Health Care organizations, including Microbiology Department of Maulana Azad Medical College and Biotechnology & Molecular Microbiology Department of the National Centre for Disease Control, New Delhi which is a premier national institute of India and is the National Reference Center for diagnosis and confirmation of different types of infectious diseases. She has over 15 publications in International Journals. She possesses extensive and in-depth knowledge of ISO15189 & CAP standards for Medical Labs. Dr. Sanjim possesses the ability to set a clear vision, execute objectives and develop winning teams of high performers who are committed to achieving exceptional results. Dr. Sanjim is known for her passion for training and development, leadership, organizational improvements, and the promotion of a “just” culture in the Lab.
